Lessons Learned

Challenges

In this project, one of my main challenges was keeping the layout consistent across all four pages while changing the content for a fictional indie game studio. Once I set up a header and footer that I could reuse it was easier to manage the site and make updates. Another challenge was just coming up with ideas based on my "company" and team.

Testing on Different Devices

I tested the site on both a laptop and a phone and in more than one browser. On a smaller screen the content stacked in a single column which made it easier to scroll through.

What I Learned Overall

Overall from everything I learned from the past semester I tried putting onto here. This wasnt the worst project because its a lot of repetition, like navigation bars, fonts, css and a lot more. So, most of this stuff we have went over from previous labs but putting it all together from previous labs and making a more professional and somewhat realistic page was cool and fun.
